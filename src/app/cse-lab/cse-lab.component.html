Computer Science and Engineering Laboratory

Well Equipped and Hi-Tech Computers are available for the use by the students and staffs as well. The labs have a 1024 Mbps connection that enables students to stay up to date with the ever changing world. The following lab experiments are conducted:


  • OS Lab (GNU/LINUX)
  • Programming Languages (C,C++)
  • Data Structures and Algorithms
  • DBMS (MySQL & PHP)
  • System Programming
  • Microprocessor & Interfacing
  • Compiler Design
  • Network Programming
